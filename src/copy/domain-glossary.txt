0. PURPOSE
----------
This glossary defines **how SSA speaks** about each domain:

- Core household domains (cooking, storehouse, garden, etc.)
- Advanced domains (butchery, curing, ferments, etc.)
- Meta domains (cycles engine, seasonal intelligence, dependencies, insights)

Use this file when:
- naming nav links, section titles, and tooltips,
- writing contextual help and empty states,
- designing new tools so they fit the **web of meaning** and not generic “modules”.

Language anchors:
- cycle, season, storehouse, yield, provision, rhythm, feast, householder, flow.
Avoid:
- task, todo, module, workload, productivity app language.

1.1 COOKING – “Cooking & Meals Rhythm”
--------------------------------------

[COOKING.ID]
"cooking"

[COOKING.LABEL_SHORT]
"Cooking & Meals"

[COOKING.LABEL_FULL]
"Cooking & Meal Rhythm"

[COOKING.ONE_LINE]
"Day-to-day meal rhythms that draw from the storehouse and welcome yields."

[COOKING.DESCRIPTION]
"Cooking in SSA is more than recipes. It is the steady rhythm of preparing
meals that draw from the storehouse, use garden and animal yields wisely,
and support feasts and everyday eating without exhausting the householder."

[COOKING.EXAMPLE_FLOWS]

- "Sabbath meal flow"
- "Breakfast-before-work rhythm"
- "Feast-day preparation cycle"

[COOKING.CORE_DATA]

- Recipes and flows
- Cooking sessions (SessionRunner)
- Links to storehouse items and yields

[COOKING.CROSS_DOMAIN]
- Pulls from: Storehouse, Garden, Animals
- Feeds: Cleaning rhythms (dishes, kitchen), Feast planning
- In cycles: garden → preservation → storehouse → meals → cleaning → compost → garden

[COOKING.SAMPLE_UI_STRINGS]

- "This cooking rhythm draws from grains, beans, and preserved vegetables."
- "A feast is approaching; consider a gentle cooking plan that honors your storehouse posture."
