0. CONCEPTUAL PURPOSE
---------------------
This file defines the **UI copy** for a page that explains STEWARDSHIP MODE:

- What Stewardship Mode is in SSA.
- How it differs from Tool Mode.
- Why a householder might turn it on.
- How it weaves cycles, seasons, yields, and storehouse posture across domains.

Tone:
- Calm, honoring, grounded in household reality.
- No shame for not using Stewardship Mode.
- Clear that this is a deeper **web of meaning** view, not a gamified “upgrade”.

The page should work as:
- A stand-alone info page reached from dashboards or “Mode” links.
- A contextual overlay or side panel when a user is invited to “See the full stewardship view”.

2. SECTION: WHAT IS STEWARDSHIP MODE?
-------------------------------------

[SECTION.WHAT_IS.TITLE]
"What is Stewardship Mode?"

[SECTION.WHAT_IS.BODY]
"Stewardship Mode is SSA’s connected view of your household life. Instead of seeing
only one tool at a time, you see how flows across domains form a living web:

- garden and animals → preservation → storehouse → meals → cleaning → compost → garden
- storehouse and feasts → rhythms of giving, mutual aid, and enterprise
- cleaning, textiles, and maintenance → spaces that guard rest and dignity

Stewardship Mode does not change your data. It changes how SSA arranges it:
from isolated tools to a cycle- and season-aware stewardship map."

[SECTION.WHAT_IS.KEY_POINT_1.TITLE]
"Cross-domain view"

[SECTION.WHAT_IS.KEY_POINT_1.BODY]
"You can see multiple domains on one dashboard: cooking, storehouse, garden,
animals, preservation, cleaning, feasts, and enterprise. Each panel reflects
flows and yields that were recorded in Tool Mode."

[SECTION.WHAT_IS.KEY_POINT_2.TITLE]
"Season & cycle intelligence"

[SECTION.WHAT_IS.KEY_POINT_2.BODY]
"Stewardship Mode highlights planting windows, harvest seasons, feast clusters,
and lean periods, and it shows how your rhythms line up with them."

[SECTION.WHAT_IS.KEY_POINT_3.TITLE]
"Storehouse posture & dependencies"

[SECTION.WHAT_IS.KEY_POINT_3.BODY]
"You can see how flows draw from or feed into your storehouse and where one
domain depends on another – for example, when enterprise or feasts might strain
provisions if garden and preservation flows are quiet."


3. SECTION: WHY TURN ON STEWARDSHIP MODE?
-----------------------------------------

[SECTION.WHY.TITLE]
"Why would a householder turn on Stewardship Mode?"

[SECTION.WHY.BODY]
"Over time, as you work with individual tools, rhythms begin to multiply.
Meals, cleaning, yields, storehouse notes, and feasts all start to form patterns.
Stewardship Mode is for the moment you begin to ask, ‘How does all of this work together?’"

[SECTION.WHY.REASON_1.TITLE]
"To see the whole household story"

[SECTION.WHY.REASON_1.BODY]
"Instead of guessing, you can see how your flows across domains combine to
support or strain the household. You see where rhythms are steady, where they
are quiet, and where a small adjustment could bring relief."

[SECTION.WHY.REASON_2.TITLE]
"To guard the storehouse"

[SECTION.WHY.REASON_2.BODY]
"Stewardship Mode lets you see, at a glance, how feasts, daily meals, preservation,
enterprise, and mutual aid draw from your provisions. This makes it easier to
plan feasts and generosity without emptying key categories."

[SECTION.WHY.REASON_3.TITLE]
"To let insights serve you"

[SECTION.WHY.REASON_3.BODY]
"When Stewardship Mode is active, SSA can offer short reflections that connect
domains, such as:

- ‘Garden yields and storehouse patterns suggest this is a good season for
   a small preservation flow.’
- ‘This enterprise rhythm may be pulling heavily from the storehouse ahead of a feast.’
- ‘Cleaning cycles may be heavy compared to your current season and energy.’"

[SECTION.WHY.CALM_NOTE]
"Stewardship Mode is an invitation, not a demand. It is here for the householder
who wants to see the web, not just the threads."

----------------------------------------------

[SECTION.PREPARATION.TITLE]
"How to prepare for Stewardship Mode (gently)"

[SECTION.PREPARATION.BODY]
"You do not need a perfect dataset. A few faithful rhythms are enough to begin."

[SECTION.PREPARATION.STEP_1.TITLE]
"Step 1 – Keep using tools as you are"

[SECTION.PREPARATION.STEP_1.BODY]
"Continue shaping and logging flows in the tools you already use:
meals, storehouse, garden, animals, cleaning, or feasts."

[SECTION.PREPARATION.STEP_2.TITLE]
"Step 2 – Add simple storehouse direction"

[SECTION.PREPARATION.STEP_2.BODY]
"When possible, mark whether a flow:
- draws from the storehouse,
- feeds into the storehouse,
- or is neutral.

This helps Stewardship Mode read your flows clearly."

[SECTION.PREPARATION.STEP_3.TITLE]
"Step 3 – Provide basic season context"

[SECTION.PREPARATION.STEP_3.BODY]
"If you share your region and approximate seasonal pattern with SSA,
it can align flows with planting windows, harvest times, and feast seasons."

[SECTION.PREPARATION.NOTE]
"Once a few flows are in place across two or more domains, Stewardship Mode
can already begin to offer useful reflections."


9. SECTION: FAQ SNIPPETS
------------------------

[SECTION.FAQ.TITLE]
"Questions about Stewardship Mode"

[SECTION.FAQ.Q1.QUESTION]
"Do I have to use Stewardship Mode for SSA to be useful?"

[SECTION.FAQ.Q1.ANSWER]
"No. Tool Mode is fully useful by itself. Stewardship Mode is an additional
way of seeing the same household data, not a requirement."

[SECTION.FAQ.Q2.QUESTION]
"Can I move back and forth between modes?"

[SECTION.FAQ.Q2.ANSWER]
"Yes. You can view a connected Stewardship overview when you want to see the whole
web, and return to Tool Mode when you want to focus on a single flow."

[SECTION.FAQ.Q3.QUESTION]
"Will Stewardship Mode change how I record flows?"

[SECTION.FAQ.Q3.ANSWER]
"Not directly. You still shape flows inside each tool. Stewardship Mode simply
reads them together and adds season, cycle, and storehouse overlays."

[SECTION.FAQ.Q4.QUESTION]
"Do I need to fill in every domain before Stewardship Mode works?"

[SECTION.FAQ.Q4.ANSWER]
"No. Even a few domains (for example, cooking, storehouse, and garden) are
enough to begin seeing meaningful patterns."

[SECTION.FAQ.Q5.QUESTION]
"Is Stewardship Mode about judging how ‘good’ my house is?"

[SECTION.FAQ.Q5.ANSWER]
"No. It is about clarity, not shame. Stewardship Mode reveals patterns so that
you can adjust rhythms in a way that protects rest, provision, and peace."

[SECTION.FAQ.Q6.QUESTION]
"Does Stewardship Mode change how sessions run?"

[SECTION.FAQ.Q6.ANSWER]
"SessionRunner still walks you through one flow at a time. In Stewardship Mode,
sessions can also emit events that update the overview panels and insights in
near real time."
